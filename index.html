Who I am

About

I'm a junior at Trinity University in San Antonio studying Business Analytics & Technology with a minor in Data Science. My real interests are in machine learning, AI engineering, and the space where data analysis meets decisions that actually matter to people.

Right now I work in IT support at Trinity, where I help a community of 3,000+ students and faculty navigate technical problems — which turns out to be a good way to learn how to communicate clearly under pressure. Outside of that I'm building projects in Python, Tableau, and Power BI, and reading things that make me think harder about what data captures and what it misses. Currently working through Invisible Women by Caroline Criado Perez.

I'm interested in AI engineering and data science roles, and I'm navigating the job market at a strange moment — one where the tools I'm learning are actively reshaping the industry I'm trying to break into. I think that's interesting, not scary.
